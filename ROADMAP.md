# HabitStack Roadmap 🗺️

Our mission is to make habit-building as intuitive as breathing. Here’s what we have planned for the future.

## 🟢 Q2 2026: Core Refinement
- [ ] **Habit Stacking**: Group habits together for better flow (e.g., "Morning Routine").
- [ ] **Push Notifications**: Browser-based reminders for daily check-ins.
- [ ] **Improved Analytics**: Monthly heatmaps and year-in-review visuals.

## 🟡 Q3 2026: Social & Gamification
- [ ] **Habit Buddies**: Share streaks with friends and compete on leaderboards.
- [ ] **Custom Rewards**: Set personal rewards for hitting streak milestones.
- [ ] **Badges V2**: More descriptive achievements and level-up animations.

## 🔴 Q4 2026: Expansion
- [ ] **Mobile App**: React Native port for iOS and Android.
- [ ] **Apple Health / Google Fit Integration**: Sync fitness habits automatically.
- [ ] **AI Coach**: Personalized advice based on completion patterns and habit failure analysis.

---
*Note: This roadmap is a living document and will evolve based on user feedback.*
